Adoption Info

First time adopting? Read this!

Step 1:

To be qualified to adopt a pet, you must:
  • Be over 21 years of age
  • Have a vet reference
  • Have the proper facilities to maintain owning a pet
  • Have the attention of adopting the pet to take care of him/her, as opposed to keeping the pet as a guard dog, research, or gift for someone else.

Step 2

Do research on what pets best suit your needs! For example, some pets are high-maintenance, or some are not kid friendly! Spotted allows you to filter your pets based on your lifestyle. Browse our pets by entering in your zipcode!




Step 3

Choose your potential pet based on its personality and needs, and set up a call with the adoption center on the pet's profile!








Step 4

Pick up your new beloved pet and enjoy the fun and love to come!
