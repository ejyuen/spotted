About Gatsby

Gatsby is a medium sized dog, weighing at 25 pounds. He has a beautiful tri-color coat, with a light undercoat. As a Corgi, he does shed a good deal a couple of times a year.

Gatsby is very active and energetic. He loves to go on hikes, play with other dogs, and play with a whole bunch of toys. Gatsby enjoys meeting new people, and loves interacting with kids. Gatsby would be happy in a bustling family environment. He would do well as the only dog in a home or in a multi-dog home.

Gatsby has good house manners and is house trained. He is in optimal health, neutered, and has had a full dental procedure. He has been vaccinated for rabies, DHLPPC (distemper, hepatitis, leptospirosis parvo, parainfluenza, and coronavirus).
