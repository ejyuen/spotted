GATSBY


Gatsby is very active and energetic. He loves to go on hikes, play with other dogs, and play with a whole bunch of toys. Gatsby enjoys meeting new people, and loves interacting with kids. Gatsby would be happy in a bustling family environment. He would do well as the only dog in a home or in a multi-dog home.

HERBERT


Herbert's an unpaired Holland Lop. Gentle and mild-mannered, Herbert can be a little aloof but loves his daily pets. He loves jumping around and climbing things, and tearing up cardboard boxes. Litter trained and socialized, Herbert would make a perfect pet for children or anyone looking for a laid back companion for their home.
PING


Choose a Siamese cat if you look forward to spending time with and interacting with your cat. This is a loyal and loving feline who will pout and pine if given little or no attention. In the right home, however, he will be a fiercely loyal companion for life.
